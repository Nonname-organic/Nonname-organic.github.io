Deep learning, a type of artificial intelligence (AI), has revolutionized various fields in recent years. Its applications in medicine, finance, and other industries have been well-documented. **ニュースの背景・問題点**
-------------------------

Physics and engineering rely heavily on data-driven approaches to understand and model complex systems. However, the sheer scale and complexity of these systems can make it challenging to identify patterns and make predictions. Deep learning, with its ability to learn from large datasets and identify subtle patterns, has the potential to revolutionize these fields.

For instance, researchers have used deep learning to analyze large datasets of medical imaging, identifying patterns that could lead to breakthroughs in disease diagnosis and treatment. Similarly, engineers have used deep learning to optimize the performance of complex systems, such as power grids and transportation networks.
